# **App Name**: Talent Track

## Core Features:

- Role-Based Login: Secure login options for TPOs, Recruiters, and Students.
- AI Resume Builder: Tool to help students create professional resumes.
- AI Resume Analysis: Analyze a student's resume for a selected job and highlight areas for improvement. Provide AI-driven feedback to students.
- Intelligent Job Matching: Suggest relevant company and job openings to students based on their resume details.
- Application Tracking: Enable students to track the status of their job applications.
- Student Performance Insights: Allow TPOs to view student information and resume scores, and suggest high-potential students.
- Recruiter Portal: Provide recruiters with suggested student profiles matching their job vacancies and enable offer letter management.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#3399FF) for a professional and trustworthy feel.
- Background color: Light cyan (#E5F5FF), offering a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Royal blue (#4169E1) for highlights and calls to action, creating visual interest.
- Font pairing: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headlines, creating a contemporary and geometric look; 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for body text, offering a humanist touch.
- Use sharp and professional icons to represent different features and actions.
- Maintain a clean and organized layout to ensure easy navigation and readability.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to enhance user engagement.
